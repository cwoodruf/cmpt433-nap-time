Nap player users guide

Nap player is a networked media player than can share media files between different nodes on a local network.

Player

Use this app to play and share media files on your nap player. You can use this to find other media files on other local players and play them.

Memos

You can record voice memos on your nap player. You can also optionally send a memo to another nap player in your local network.

Intercom

Alert a family member or colleague near another nap player with the intercom feature. If they are not available you can leave a message for them.

When you leave a message with the intercom or send a memo the nap player will set an led blinking to indicate that there is a message available there.
